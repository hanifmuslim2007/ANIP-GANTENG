About Me

Hi! My name is Hanif. I’m a passionate powerlifting athlete who has competed in three official competitions. In 2024, I proudly earned two 2nd place medals, and in 2025, I joined another competition where I didn’t win but successfully qualified for Porda (Pekan olahraga daerah). These experiences taught me the importance of hard work, mental strength, and discipline, shaping me into someone who never gives up easily. Beyond sports, I also manage a small business selling Roblox game items, which started as a hobby but grew into a valuable learning experience about entrepreneurship, marketing, and customer trust. Balancing athletics and business has helped me develop strong time management, leadership, and problem-solving skills that I continuously apply in every aspect of my life.
